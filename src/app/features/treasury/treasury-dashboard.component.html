This dashboard is organized around 4 questions a Treasury and Real Estate team would ask in a quarterly portfolio review. Each tab answers one question with only the data that serves it — no competing context.

Tab 1 groups 3 things on purpose. KPIs, state utilization, and predictive flags all answer "where are we exposed?" Together they complete the picture: KPIs give scale, the bar chart gives geography, the flags table gives the specific assets that need a decision. Separately, they're just charts.

Tab 4 is separated deliberately. T&E data comes from a different source — synthetic travel records rather than FRPP real estate data. Keeping it on its own tab signals that this is a distinct analytical lens, not an extension of the portfolio view.

Predictive methodology: 2-period linear trend extrapolation on utilization rate. The 60% threshold is sourced from OMB M-25-25 (USE IT Act, April 2025), applied here as a corporate portfolio review benchmark.

Anomaly detection: 1.5σ rolling 6-month window applied to monthly T&E spend by category. Months exceeding the threshold are marked as anomalies worth investigation.

In production, this connects to Workday, Coupa, and Snowflake with automated refresh and threshold-breach alerting.
